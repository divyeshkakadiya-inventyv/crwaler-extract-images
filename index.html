About Fusion Polymer

Small Business, Big on Quality

We're a small, family-owned polymer business based in Gujarat, India. Started in 2004 by experienced rubber technologists, we specialize in custom rubber extrusion and molding for local and regional industries.

What makes us different? Personal service. When you call us, you talk to people who actually make your products. We're small enough to be flexible and quick, but experienced enough to handle complex requirements. Over 20 years, we've built lasting relationships with our clients by focusing on quality and reliability.
